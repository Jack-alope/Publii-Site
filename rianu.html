Rianu

Updated on August 16, 2023

Rianú is an open-source software, released under the BSD 3-Clause License, designed to more efficiently track and analyze engineered cardiac tissues. 

A journal article is available at https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cmpbup.2023.100107.

Documentation is available at https://rianu.mrph.dev.

Code is available at https://gitlab.com/hect-software/rianu.
