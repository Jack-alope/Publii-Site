Saor Crimp

Updated on August 16, 2023
 

This was a project carried out as part of the engineering coursework at Trinity College Dublin. We were tasked with designing a medical device using 3D printing technologies. We chose to design was a stent crimper that could be easily 3D printed and used on a lab bench or in the cath lab.

Project website is available at https://stent.mrph.dev

CAD files are available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.8174111
